FAQ

Common Questions

How does FishLogic know where I should fish?

▼

FishLogic combines real-time weather data, water temperature trends, barometric pressure, and fish behavior science to identify where fish are most likely holding on your lake right now. Every recommendation includes a confidence score so you know how strong the pattern is.

What species does FishLogic cover?

▼

Largemouth bass, smallmouth bass, spotted bass, striped bass, and crappie. Each species has its own behavior model so guidance is tailored to how that fish responds to current conditions.

Does this actually work, or is it just a weather app?

▼

Weather apps tell you the forecast. FishLogic tells you what to do with it — which structure to target, what depth to fish, which lure to throw, what color, and how to retrieve it. It's the difference between knowing it's 54 degrees and knowing that means bass are staging on secondary points in 8-12 feet and you should throw a jerkbait on a slow retrieve.

Is there a free trial?

▼

Yes — 3 days of full access, no restrictions. Pick your lake, get your guidance, and go fish. If it's not for you, cancel before the trial ends and you won't be charged.

Will other people see my fishing data?

▼

No. FishLogic is not a social app. There are no feeds, no followers, no sharing features, and no way for other users to see your activity. Your spots stay yours.

How often does the guidance update?

▼

Every morning at 6 AM local time. Guidance is generated fresh daily based on the latest weather conditions, temperature trends, and seasonal patterns for your lake.
