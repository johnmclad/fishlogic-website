Common Questions

Is this a social app?

No. FishLogic has no social features. No feeds, no followers, no sharing. Your spots stay private.

How accurate is the guidance?

Every recommendation includes a confidence score. On high-confidence days (80%+), conditions strongly match proven patterns. We tell you when conditions are tough, too.

What lakes and species are covered?

176 lakes across 15 states. Largemouth bass, smallmouth bass, spotted bass, striped bass, and crappie. More added regularly.

Can I try it before paying?

Yes. 3-day free trial with full access. No credit card required.

How is this different from a weather app?

Weather apps show conditions. FishLogic tells you what to do with those conditions: which structure to target, what depth, and which lure based on how fish respond.
