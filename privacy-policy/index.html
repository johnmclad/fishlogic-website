Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 11, 2026

1. Introduction

FishLogic ("we," "us," "our," or the "App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal data.

By using the App, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree to this policy, you must not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account using Apple Sign-In or Google Sign-In, we receive and store:

  • Your email address (as provided by Apple or Google)
  • Your display name (as provided by Apple or Google)
  • A unique user identifier
  • Account creation timestamp

We do not receive or store your Apple ID or Google account password. Authentication is handled securely through Apple's Sign-In service or Google's OAuth 2.0 service.

2.2 Subscription Data

We collect and store information about your subscription status:

  • Subscription tier (Single State or Nationwide)
  • Subscription status (trialing, active, canceled, expired)
  • Billing plan (monthly or annual)
  • Selected state (for Single State subscriptions)
  • Trial start and end dates
  • Subscription history (upgrades, downgrades, conversions)

Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. We do not receive or store your credit card number, billing address, or any other payment details. Apple provides us only with confirmation of your subscription status.

2.3 Fishing Guidance Requests

When you request fishing guidance, we process:

  • The lake you selected
  • Current environmental conditions (weather, temperature, pressure)
  • Your user preferences (if provided)

This data is sent to our AI service provider (Anthropic) to generate personalized fishing recommendations. Anthropic processes this data according to their privacy policy and does not retain or train on your data.

2.4 Catch Logging (Optional)

You may optionally log catches to track your fishing success. All catch data is completely optional. When you choose to log a catch, you may provide:

  • Fish species (from a predefined list)
  • Weight and length measurements
  • Depth where the fish was caught
  • GPS coordinates (only if you tap "Capture Current Location")
  • Lake where the fish was caught
  • Lure or bait used
  • Photos (from your camera or photo library)
  • Notes about the catch

Your catch data is private. Catch logs are stored in your account and are never shared publicly, with other users, or used for social features. This data is used solely to help you track your personal fishing history. Photos are stored securely and are only visible to you.

You can delete any catch log at any time, and all associated data (including photos and GPS coordinates) will be permanently removed.

2.5 Weather and Environmental Data

We fetch weather data from Open-Meteo and World Weather Online Marine API based on lake coordinates to provide fishing guidance. This includes air and water temperature, barometric pressure, wind conditions, and marine data. This is environmental data about locations, not personal data about you.

2.6 Usage Analytics

We automatically collect certain information to improve the App:

  • Lakes you view and select
  • Features you use (guidance, maps, settings)
  • App open frequency and session duration
  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version
  • Crash reports and error logs

2.7 Service Usage Data

We track the number and timing of guidance requests per user to enforce fair usage limits, prevent abuse, and improve service quality. This includes:

  • Request timestamps and frequency
  • Lakes and species queried
  • AI processing metrics (token usage)

This data is used solely for service operation, cost management, and abuse prevention. It is not shared with third parties or used for advertising purposes.

2.8 Push Notification Data

If you enable push notifications, we store a device token to send you notifications about fishing conditions, weather alerts, and optimal fishing windows. You can disable notifications at any time in your device Settings or within the App.

2.9 Location Data

Location access is optional. If you grant location permission, we use it to:

  • Sort lakes by distance from your current location
  • Provide more relevant lake recommendations

We do NOT track your location in the background, record your fishing locations, or share your location with third parties. You can deny location permission and still use all core App features.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide personalized AI-powered fishing guidance
  • Process and manage your subscription
  • Send push notifications (if enabled)
  • Improve our AI recommendation algorithms
  • Analyze app performance and fix bugs
  • Respond to customer support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do NOT use your information for targeted advertising. We do NOT sell your personal data.

4. AI Processing and Data

FishLogic uses artificial intelligence (Claude by Anthropic) to generate fishing recommendations. When you request guidance:

  • Environmental data (weather, lake structure) is sent to Anthropic's API
  • Anthropic processes this data to generate recommendations
  • Anthropic does not retain your data or use it for training
  • No personally identifiable information is included in AI requests

For more information about Anthropic's data practices, please visit anthropic.com/privacy.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your account data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database provider with:

  • Encryption at rest and in transit (TLS 1.2+)
  • SOC 2 Type II compliance
  • Regular security audits
  • Row-level security policies

We implement industry-standard security measures including JWT authentication, rate limiting, and input validation. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. How We Share Your Information

We never sell your personal data. We do not share your information with advertisers, data brokers, or social media platforms.

We may share data with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party services that help us operate the App (Supabase for data storage, Anthropic for AI processing, Open-Meteo and World Weather Online for weather data). These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for providing services to us.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, subpoena, or government request, or to protect our legal rights.
  • Business Transfers: If we merge with, are acquired by, or sell assets to another company, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you of any such change.
  • With Your Consent: We may share information for other purposes if you provide explicit consent.

7. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Supabase: Authentication and database - supabase.com/privacy
  • Apple Sign-In: Authentication - apple.com/legal/privacy
  • Google Sign-In: Authentication - policies.google.com/privacy
  • Anthropic (Claude): AI processing - anthropic.com/privacy
  • Open-Meteo: Air temperature and conditions - open-meteo.com/en/terms
  • World Weather Online: Water temperature - worldweatheronline.com/privacy
  • Apple Maps: In-app maps - apple.com/legal/privacy
  • Apple Push Notifications: Notifications - apple.com/legal/privacy
  • Amazon Associates: Affiliate product links - amazon.com/gp/help/customer/display.html?nodeId=468496

When you tap affiliate links in the App (such as "Buy on Amazon" or "Shop on Amazon"), you are redirected to Amazon.com. We do not collect or store any data about your purchases. Amazon's own privacy policy governs your interaction with their site. FishLogic earns a small commission from qualifying purchases at no additional cost to you.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data as follows:

  • Account data: Retained while your account is active
  • Subscription history: Retained for 7 years for tax/legal compliance
  • Usage analytics: Aggregated and anonymized after 2 years
  • Catch logs: Retained until you delete them or your account (including photos and GPS data)

If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where required by law to retain it (e.g., financial records for tax purposes).

9. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Delete your account and associated data
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Disable push notifications at any time
  • Withdraw Consent: Revoke location or notification permissions

To exercise these rights, use the account deletion feature in the App (Account → Delete Account) or contact us at support@fishlogicapp.com.

10. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to Know: Request disclosure of categories and specific pieces of personal information collected in the past 12 months.
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale: We do not sell your personal information.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights.

To submit a California privacy request, email support@fishlogicapp.com with subject "California Privacy Request."

11. European Union and UK Users (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union, European Economic Area, or the United Kingdom, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and UK GDPR apply to our processing of your personal data.

Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Contract Performance: Processing necessary to provide you with the App and subscription services you requested.
  • Legitimate Interests: Processing necessary for our legitimate business interests, such as improving the App, preventing fraud, and ensuring security.
  • Consent: Processing based on your explicit consent, such as optional location access, push notifications, and optional catch logging. You may withdraw consent at any time.
  • Legal Obligations: Processing necessary to comply with applicable laws.

Your GDPR Rights

Under GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right of Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Right to Restriction: Request restriction of processing in certain circumstances.
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests at any time.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent.
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: File a complaint with your local data protection supervisory authority.

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@fishlogicapp.com. We will respond within 30 days as required by GDPR.

12. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States and other countries where our service providers operate.

For EU/UK Users: When we transfer personal data outside the EU/EEA/UK, we implement appropriate safeguards including Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission and the UK International Data Transfer Agreement where applicable.

13. Children's Privacy

FishLogic is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age (or 16 in the European Union). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have collected data from a child under the applicable age, we will delete it promptly.

14. Do Not Track

Some browsers have a "Do Not Track" feature. Because there is no common standard for interpreting DNT signals, our App does not currently respond to DNT signals.

Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
